Foreign Lawyer Notes

Indonesian law firm
There are advocates, consultant advocates, foreign advocates.
Advocate refers to Indonesia lawyers who practice as litigators/barristers.
Other practitioners are referred to as lawyers or consultants.
Foreign lawyers cannot practise as litigators and may be consultants only. Foreign lawyers are qualified to practise on the basis of a foreign practising certificate which must be current.
PERADI is responsible for regulation of legal profession - they recommend employment of the foreign lawyer.

A foreign lawyer must be:
Employed by indonesian law firm; and
Submit copies of foreign legal qualifications and current practising certificate and law society membership to PERADI and ministry of law.

Foreign lawyer obliged to transfer legal knowledge to Indo lawyers and provide free legal services to education, legal research and government institutions for at least 10 hours a month.

1 foreign lawyer for every 4 employed Indo lawyers.
BUT a firm with 3 lawyers may employ a foreigner.
